# WINSTEP — Design Brief

## Design read
For endurance athletes (triathletes/Ironman) who stress their feet more than anyone and care about natural recovery. Emotional register: grounded, determined, premium-natural — the calm after the effort.

## Concept spine
"The recovery ritual" — the site is the moment after the race, where the athlete cares for their feet as deliberately as they trained. Every section mirrors the arc: stress > application > relief > return.

## Delivery tier
Cinema — marketing/brand site with scroll-driven motion, parallax hero, staggered reveals.

## Locked palette
- Primary dark: `#0A2E36` (deep teal — endurance water, deep nature)
- Accent: `#D4FF2E` (acid lime — energy, the "win", recovery spark)
- Light bg: `#F5F1E8` (warm ivory — natural, skin, balm)
- Secondary: `#1A4A4A` (lighter teal for cards/sections)
- Text on dark: `#E8E4D8` (warm off-white)
- Text muted: `#7A8B8B` (grey-teal)

Defense: deep teal grounds the brand in nature and endurance (water/trail), acid lime is the athletic energy spark that no competitor uses, warm ivory replaces clinical white with organic warmth. No banned palette families.

## Locked type
- Display/headlines: system bold condensed uppercase (sporty, athletic, no external font)
- Body: system sans-serif (clean, readable, fast)

## Tier-1 technique
Scroll-parallax hero — the hero image moves at a different rate than the content as the user scrolls, creating depth and a sense of forward motion. Paired with staggered text reveals on scroll-in for each section.

## Section plan
1. Hero — full-bleed image, bold headline, dual CTA (parallax scroll)
2. Problem — "Tre discipline, un solo punto di contatto" (text + image split)
3. Product — product showcase with key benefits (image + features grid)
4. Ingredients — natural formula grid (6 ingredient cards)
5. Testimonial — Damiano quote (lifestyle image + quote)
6. Pricing — three offers (single, kit, subscription)
7. FAQ — accordion (5 items)
8. Final CTA + Footer

## Asset plan
- Hero image: cinematic endurance recovery (16:9)
- Product image: studio tube shot (1:1)
- Ingredients flat lay: natural ingredients (4:3)
- Lifestyle: athlete applying product (3:2)
- App branding: cover + icon (via generate_app_branding)
- All downloaded into `app/public/assets/`
